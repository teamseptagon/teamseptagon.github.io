Team Septagon

Team Charter

Hello and welcome to our website. We are a philanthropic organization operating in the Bloomington Illinois area. Our group was formed because of a class for school. For our Communications 223: Small Group Processes class, we are required to form a group, complete a goal, and learn about small group development at the same time. For our goal, we chose to work with a local non-profit charity, Home Sweet Home Ministries. Home Sweet Home Ministries provides numerous services to the under-served in our community. This group provides a shelter, dining center, pantry, and so much more. Our goal was to assist with the 2017 Run For Hope Charity Run, a charity run in which all proceeds go to the charity.
